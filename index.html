About

Our Mandate

The Association of Mine Managers of Zimbabwe provides a platform for mining industry professionals to discuss, evaluate and debate

  • mining and minerals policy
  • technical innovations
  • matters of management of mines and recommendations suggested
  • safety and health challenges
  • advancements in mining operations in the country.

The AMMZ has been in existence for the past 48 years, and was formed in 1972 to advance the science and practice of Mining and supporting disciplines such as Survey, Geology, and Metallurgy. It is a vehicle for information exchange and dissemination on good practices and seeks to promote the study and growth of Mining and allied disciplines. The AMMZ is an affiliate of the Chamber of Mines of Zimbabwe

Here are our planned dates for the Annual General Meetings and Council meetings.

Council Meetings occur monthly and the AGM takes place once a year as per the name.

  • Annual General Meeting - 19 November 2021
  • Executice Council meetings - Last Friday of every month

The main agendas for the annual general meetings would be to follow up on the minutes from the previous meeting to see if the discussed things where done, to elect a new Presidium and vote for new executive council.

The anuual conference is a yearly event the AMMZ hosts.

Its meant to bring together over two hundred mine managers and executives who drive over 90% of the country’s mineral production. In addition, the conference will include captains of industry in the local and international mining landscape
